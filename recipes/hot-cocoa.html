Hot Cocoa

A creamy cup of chocolate goodness.

Perfect for cold nights and cozying up, hot cocoa is a staple of the cold seasons.

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cups half-and-half
