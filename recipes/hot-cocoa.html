Hot Cocoa

A creamy cup of chocolate goodness.

Perfect for cold nights and cozying up, hot cocoa is a staple of the cold seasons.

Serves 4, and works great with toppings such as marshmallows, whipped cream, and chocolate drizzle.

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup white sugar (or to liking)
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅓ cup water
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cups half-and-half
  • Toppings of choice (optional)

Steps:

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a sauce pan
  2. Bring water to a boil, and stir it into sauce pan
  3. Simmer the mixture for 2 minutes while stirring continuously
  4. Add milk and heat up mixture without boiling
  5. Once heated, remove milk from heat and stir in vanilla
  6. Serve in mugs with half-and-half
  7. Add toppings of choice and enjoy
