Ingredients:

Dressing:
  • 3 anchovy fillets
  • anchovy fillets
  • ½ lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 large egg yolk
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
Salad:
  • ½ head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons croutons

Steps:

Dressing:
  1. Mash anchovy fillets and garlic in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add lemon juice, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, egg yolk, and Worcestershire sauce, and whisk until smooth and creamy.
  3. Gradually stream in olive oil while whisking constantly.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
Salad:
  1. Gently mix romaine lettuce and Parmesan cheese into dressing until thoroughly coated
  2. Serve salad topped with croutons
